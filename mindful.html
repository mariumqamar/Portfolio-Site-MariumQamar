Project Introduction

What is Mindful?

Mindful is a meditation app where users can create a personalised meditation experience with the app adjusting their experience according to their mood.

The problem

Research suggests that many meditation apps do not offer personalised meditation experiences or mood journal logs, which can be a barrier to building a consistent practice. Without tracking progress and understanding the impact on well-being, users may struggle to stay engaged and motivated.

The solution

By incorporating personalised meditation experiences and mood journaling features, meditation apps have the potential to provide users with a more tailored and effective mindfulness practice. This could lead to greater user engagement and ultimately, more significant improvements in mental health and well-being.

User Flow

A user flow chart is a tool that maps out paths our users will take when navigating through our product to achieve a certain goal.

By visualising this process, designers can scrutinise and refine each step and interaction in the user journey to optimise the user experience.

Wireframing

Wireframes were created in different fidelities using figma for IOS and android. IOS screens were designed following the human interface guidelines and the android screens were designed using material design principles.

Usability Testing

Usability testing was conducted with six participants.

Results:

  • Feedback was generally positive
  • Participants could not see the current sound player

Iterations:

  • Current sound player was changed to green to pass accessibility tests.

Features 01.

Onboarding

Users will answer a few questions to tailor their meditation experience to their needs.

Features 02.

My Plan

A meditation plan that gets updated according to the users mood log.

Features 03.

Mood

A daily mood log and journal entry for users to enter their thoughts and feelings.

Features 04.

Sounds

Users can listen to meditative sounds and sounds based on their mood will be recommended.

Style Guide

Colours

Pink is often associated with feelings of calm, comfort, and tranquility. It can also evoke a sense of nurturing and caring, as it is often associated with feminine qualities. Green, on the other hand, is often associated with nature, growth, and renewal. It can evoke a sense of balance and harmony, as well as feelings of calmness and relaxation. The combination of pink and green creates a calming and nurturing environment, promoting feelings of tranquility, balance, and growth.

Style Guide

Logo

Fonts
